# Plans & billing

Tally starts with a free trial, then a simple subscription. Every plan includes **all** of Tally —
the only difference is how often you're billed.

## Free trial

You get **21 days free** to try Tally with your own expenses. No commitment — log real expenses, ask
questions, run an export, and see if it fits how you work.

## Plans

All three plans are the same product. Pick the billing rhythm you prefer:

| Plan | Price | Works out to |
|------|-------|--------------|
| **Weekly** | $5.99 / week | — |
| **Monthly** | $12.49 / month | — |
| **Annual** | $119.88 / year | ≈ $9.99 / month — **save ~20%** |

The **Annual** plan is the best value if you're planning to track all year (which, for taxes, you
probably are).

::: tip Sharing with a partner
A plan covers you and can include one co-owner (like a spouse or business partner) on the same
subscription. Larger teams aren't supported yet.
:::

## Managing your subscription

Everything billing-related lives in your [dashboard](/guide/dashboard) under Settings:

- Start or change your plan
- Update your payment method
- View invoices
- Cancel anytime

Payments are handled securely by Stripe — Tally never stores your card details.
