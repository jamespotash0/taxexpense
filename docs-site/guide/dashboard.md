# The dashboard

Texting is how you *capture* expenses. The web dashboard is where you *review* them — see everything
in one place, fix anything, and get your records out at tax time.

## Signing in

Go to [tallywhy.com](https://tallywhy.com) and sign in with your phone number. We'll text you a
6-digit code — enter it and you're in. (Same simple login as when you signed up; no password to
remember.)

## What you can do

- **See all your expenses** — every one you've texted, with vendor, amount, date, category, and the
  tax code section it falls under.
- **Edit anything** — fix an amount, change a category, add or update the business purpose, mark
  something business or personal.
- **Spot what needs attention** — expenses still missing a receipt or context are flagged so you can
  finish them off.
- **Add receipts** — attach a photo to an expense.
- **Export your records** — download a CSV or QuickBooks file, or email everything to your
  accountant. See [Exporting & your accountant](/guide/exporting).
- **Manage settings** — your accountant's email, your defaults, and your plan.

## Texting vs. dashboard

| Task | Best done by… |
|------|---------------|
| Logging an expense in the moment | **Text** |
| A quick correction to the last one | **Text** |
| Editing an older expense | **Dashboard** |
| Bulk review / cleanup | **Dashboard** |
| Exporting or emailing your accountant | **Dashboard** |
