# Privacy & security

Your expense records are sensitive, and Tally treats them that way.

## Your data is yours

- **Private by default.** Your expenses and receipt photos are visible only to you through your
  account. They aren't public, and they aren't shared.
- **Receipt photos are stored securely** in private storage and served through short-lived, signed
  links — not open URLs anyone could stumble onto.
- **You're in control.** Every expense is editable, and you can export your full records at any time.

## Signing in

Tally uses your **phone number** plus a one-time 6-digit code to sign in — there's no password to
leak or reuse. Codes expire quickly and are limited to a few attempts.

## Payments

Billing is handled by **Stripe**, an industry-standard payments provider. Tally never sees or stores
your full card number.

## Text-message consent

Because Tally works over SMS, we only message you with your consent, and you can **opt out at any
time** by replying STOP. We keep a record of your consent to stay compliant with messaging
regulations.

## A note on tax advice

Tally keeps your records — it is **not** tax advice, and it doesn't file anything with the IRS. For
questions about what's deductible or what you'll owe, please consult a CPA.
