# Fixing & correcting

Got something wrong, or want to add a detail after the fact? Just text Tally — it updates the most
recent expense for you. No need to delete and re-enter.

## Correct a detail

Text the fix right after the expense and Tally applies it:

> actually that was $52, not $42
>
> the date was May 2
>
> that one was personal
>
> the vendor was Sweetgreen

## Add context later

You can also fill in the *why* a little after logging:

> that lunch was with a client about the Q3 deal
>
> the dinner was for my own team
>
> Tabernacle is a restaurant

Tally attaches the detail to the expense you just logged — it won't create a duplicate.

## Which expense does it change?

A correction applies to your **most recent** expense (within a short window). If Tally isn't sure
which one you mean, it'll ask.

For older expenses, or to change several at once, use the **dashboard**, where you can edit any
expense directly. See [The dashboard](/guide/dashboard).

## Flag one for your accountant

Not sure how something should be treated? Ask Tally to flag it for your CPA:

> flag the $48 lunch

It marks that expense so it stands out on your export, and your accountant can weigh in. If you have
several similar ones, Tally will ask which to flag.
