# FAQ

Quick answers to the questions people ask most. If yours isn't here, just text Tally "can you…?" and
it'll tell you.

## Getting started

### Do I need to download an app?
No. Tally works entirely over text messaging. To review or export, you sign in to the web dashboard
in your browser.

### How do I sign in?
With your phone number and a 6-digit code we text you. No password.

## Logging expenses

### Do I have to send a receipt photo every time?
No. Most expenses are logged from a text alone. Tally asks for a photo only when the rules require it
— mainly strict-category expenses of $75+ and lodging. See
[When Tally asks for more](/guide/substantiation).

### Can I set the date on an expense?
Yes. Mention the date ("$40 lunch on June 1") or say "yesterday." If you send a receipt photo, Tally
uses the date on the receipt. Otherwise it defaults to the day you text.

### Does it handle mileage?
Yes — text the distance and purpose ("drove 22 miles to a client meeting"). See
[Mileage](/guide/mileage).

### Can it read a photo of a receipt?
Yes. Snap a picture and text it; Tally reads the vendor, amount, and date. See
[Receipts & photos](/guide/receipts-and-photos).

### I made a mistake — can I fix it?
Yes. Text a correction right after ("actually that was $52", "that one was personal"), or edit it in
the dashboard. See [Fixing & correcting](/guide/corrections).

### Can I log several expenses at once?
Send each as its own message for the cleanest record. A few in a row is fine — just keep them
separate.

## Reviewing & exporting

### Can I ask Tally how much I've spent?
Yes — "how much on meals this year?", "show my last 3 charges", "review my year." See
[Asking questions](/guide/asking-questions).

### Can I export to QuickBooks?
Yes. Download a QuickBooks-compatible file (or a standard CSV) from your dashboard. See
[Exporting & your accountant](/guide/exporting).

### Can I send my records to my accountant?
Yes. Add their email in Settings, then email your records in one tap from the dashboard.

## Taxes

### Does Tally give tax advice?
No. Tally keeps your records and categorizes expenses with the relevant tax code section, but it
doesn't tell you what you owe or whether something is deductible. For that, talk to a CPA.

### Does Tally file my taxes?
No. Tally organizes your records so filing (by you, your software, or your accountant) is easier — it
doesn't file anything itself.

### What's the deal with the "why"?
Your bank knows *what* you spent; it doesn't know *why*. Capturing the business reason in the
moment — "client dinner, Q3 deal" — is what makes an expense documentation-complete and easy to
defend. That's Tally's whole job.

## Account & billing

### Is there a free trial?
Yes — 21 days. See [Plans & billing](/guide/billing).

### What does it cost?
Weekly $5.99, Monthly $12.49, or Annual $119.88 (≈$9.99/mo). All plans include everything. See
[Plans & billing](/guide/billing).

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, from your dashboard.

### Can my spouse or partner share my account?
A plan can include one co-owner. Larger teams aren't supported yet.

## Privacy

### Who can see my expenses?
Only you, through your account. See [Privacy & security](/guide/privacy-security).

### How do I stop receiving texts?
Reply STOP at any time.
