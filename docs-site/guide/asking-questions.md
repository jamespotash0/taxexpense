# Asking questions by text

Tally isn't just for logging — you can ask it about what you've already tracked, right from your
messages.

## What you can ask

**Totals**

> how much have I spent on meals this year?
>
> what's my total for software last quarter?

**Breakdowns by category**

> show me a breakdown of this month
>
> where did my money go this year?

**Recent expenses**

> show my last 3 charges
>
> what did I log recently?

**A year review**

> review my year

Tally replies with the numbers, pulled straight from your records.

## Commands you can text

> export
>
> email my accountant

These point you to your [dashboard](/guide/dashboard), where the actual export or email happens in a
tap.

## About tax questions

Tally tracks your records — it isn't a tax advisor. So if you ask something like "how much will I
owe?" or "is this deductible?", it won't guess. It'll gently point you to a CPA and offer to show
what you've logged instead.

> **You:** will I owe taxes on this?
>
> **Tally:** I keep your records, not tax advice — for what you'll owe or whether something's
> deductible, check with a CPA. But I can tell you what you've logged: try "how much on meals this
> year?" or "review my year".

## "Can you…?" questions

Not sure whether Tally can do something? Just ask:

> can I set the date on an expense?
>
> do you read receipts?
>
> can I export to QuickBooks?

Tally will tell you straight.
